Guest on Radio Free XP Podcast

I guested on a podcast!

Davis W. Frank · 28 October 2023 · under 1 minute to read ·

For your weekend podcast listening pleasure - I was the guest on Radio Free XP this week1.

I got to talk to Tony and Jesse about my Pairing Journey. There is probably the most resonance with folks who worked at or with Pivotal/Labs, but I got to tell some fun stories. We talked about parallels between pairing and collaborative gaming - including a shout-out to the classic Infocom text adventures and the more recent Overcooked - and pairing stories from the real world that worked and didn’t. Then we wind up by talking to our former coworkers who are impacted by the Broadcom/VMware acquisition.

Share and enjoy!
